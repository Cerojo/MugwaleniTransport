About Us It's Nice to Meet You!

Transport and Logistics CC (Mugwaleni Transport) is a family owned business which was founded by Mr. Ronard Mathevula in 2006. Mr. Mathevula started the business as an independent contractor with one truck. Within three years the business had grown, with two additional trucks being added to the fleet. Today twelve years after the start of the company, Mugwaleni Transport has a fleet of fifteen (15) trucks and a staff of 22 people.

Our company provides an expedited, daily door to door trucking road haulage service throughout South Africa, hauling from one tonne up to thirty two (32) tonne loads. We support haulage that includes machinery, drums and industrial equipment etc, to and from all main hubs, warehouses and being forwarded to all outlying towns using all routes.

Mission

Mugwaleni Transport aims to provide transportation service that is safe, reliable and efficient by utilizing quality personnel and resources; offer cost effective environmentally friendly and problem- solving solution that assist our customers in achieving their objectives. A company that will boast of having some of the best and most reliable truck drivers in the whole nation
